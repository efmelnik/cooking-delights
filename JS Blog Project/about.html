About Us

At Cooking Delights, we are dedicated to celebrating the culinary richness of Belgium. Our platform serves as a curated space for food enthusiasts, home cooks, and curious palates to discover the traditional and modern flavors of Belgian cuisine. Through authentic recipes, cultural insights, and practical cooking tips, we aim to preserve culinary heritage while making Belgian dishes approachable for a global audience. Whether it's exploring the bold flavors of Carbonnade Flamande or the artistry of Belgian chocolate, our purpose is to inspire a deeper appreciation for food, culture, and community.




Meet Ehud (Site Creator)

Ehud Melnik is a junior at the University of South Carolina, majoring in Middle Level Education with a minor in Integrated Information Technology. As a native of Myrtle Beach, SC, Ehud brings a unique blend of southern hospitality and international curiosity to Cooking Delights. Growing up in a coastal town known for its vibrant tourism and diverse food scenes, Ehud developed an early appreciation for how culture and cuisine intertwine. Now, Ehud combines his love for learning and tech to explore and share the rich culinary traditions of Belgium with fellow food enthusiasts.
